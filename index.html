Mahabodhi Prakriti Buddha Vihara

Mahabodhi Prakriti Buddha Vihara, is a branch of Mahabodhi Society Bengaluru, located in the serene outskirts of Bengaluru, Karnataka, continues its journey as a non-profit organization dedicated to spiritual development in sync with the natural surroundings. In the past year, we have made significant progress toward our mission, and we are pleased to share the highlights of our accomplishments.

1. Spiritual Sanctuary

Our Vihara boasts a beautiful temple dedicated to puja and meditation. This sacred space has been a center for spiritual activities and communal gatherings, further strengthening our spiritual community.

Puja on the occassion of Bada Bhante, Acharya Buddharakkhita's SramanjaliA group photo

2. Monastic Community

Mahabodhi Prakriti Buddha Vihara is blessed with a vibrant monastic community, including 1 Bhikkhu, 2 Samaneras, and 1 Anagarika, all dedicated to a life of spiritual practice and service. In addition, we have 1 staff member who plays a vital role in supporting our daily operations.

Venerable Tiloka gifting to monksSamaneras receiving the gifts

3. Infrastruckture Developement

Over the past year, we have made strides in enhancing our facilities. We have successfully constructed two kutis (meditation huts) and a dedicated bathroom for Bhikkhus, providing a tranquil space for meditation and reflection amidst nature's beauty.

4. Center Activities

In alignment with our mission of harmony with nature, we have actively engaged in environmental activities. These include the plantation of trees, vegetables, and flowers within our Vihara premises. This not only contributes to the ecological balance but also provides a serene and meditative atmosphere for our residents and visitors.

5. Leadership

We are honored to have Venerable Bhikkhu Tiloka as the dedicated and compassionate center incharge. His wisdom and guidance continue to inspire and lead our community towards spiritual growth.

5. Outreach and Future Plans

While we have made significant strides, we remain committed to ongoing development. Our vision includes expanding our outreach programs, organizing spiritual retreats, and further enhancing our infrastructure to accommodate more seekers.
